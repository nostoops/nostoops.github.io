Being from San Diego, I spend most of my free time paddling out, climbing, or hiking with friends.

Currently as an Environmental Planner for ICF, Inc., I assist with energy, infrastructure, and environmental planning projects and conduct environmental analysis in accordance with applicable regulations (e.g., CEQA, NEPA). Project deliverables include EIRs, CEs, IS/MNDs, NOEs, and other documents that are required for submission to local and state regulatory agencies. As a GIS Analyst, I contribute to the GIS workload by preparing custom map figures, web apps, shapefiles, graphics, and reports in support of engineering and environmental consulting projects using Esri ArcGIS, related extensions, relational databases, and other software. I primarily use ArcMap, ArcGIS Pro, ArcGIS Online, ArcGIS Portal, and ArcGIS Enterprise for spatial analyses, data management, and data visualization. My duties include extracting and converting non-GIS data into GIS formats, performing spatial analyses using a variety of geoprocessing tools, symbolizing features, summarizing GIS calculations, and using ArcGIS, Python, SQL, and other software to obtain and/or correct spatial and attribute data, automate analysis, and QA/QC relational databases. I also provided support, troubleshooting, and maintenance for existing GIS systems and databases, applications, scripts, tools, and web services.
